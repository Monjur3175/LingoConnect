Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and basic demographic information. This helps us personalize your learning experience and provide relevant content.

Learning Data

We track your learning progress, session duration, and lesson attendance to improve our services and provide personalized recommendations.

Payment Information

Payment details are processed securely through third-party payment processors. We do not store complete credit card numbers on our servers.

Technical Data

We collect device information, IP addresses, and browser data to ensure platform compatibility and security.

How We Use Your Information

Service Delivery

We use your information to provide language learning services, connect you with teachers, and personalize your learning experience.

Communication

We send important updates, lesson reminders, and promotional content via email and in-app notifications.

Analytics

We analyze usage patterns to improve our platform, develop new features, and optimize the learning experience.

Information Sharing

With Teachers

We share necessary information with your assigned teachers to facilitate lessons and track progress. Teachers can only access information relevant to their teaching.

Third Parties

We may share anonymized, aggregated data with trusted partners for research and platform improvement purposes.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and property.

Data Security

Encryption

All data transmissions are encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols to protect your information during transmission.

Storage

Personal and payment data are stored on secure servers with limited access and regular security audits.

Access Control

Only authorized personnel can access your personal information, and all access is logged for security purposes.

Your Rights

Access and Correction

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time through your account settings.

Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format for personal use or transfer to other services.

Opt-Out

You can opt out of marketing communications and certain data collection activities while maintaining access to core services.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@lingoconnect.com

Address: 123 Learning Street, Education City, EC 12345

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting, and we will notify users of significant updates via email.
