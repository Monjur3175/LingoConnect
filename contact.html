Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I get started with LingoConnect?

Simply sign up for a free account, browse our available tutors, and book your first lesson. We offer a trial lesson for new students to help you find the perfect match.

What languages do you offer?

We offer over 50 languages including Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Russian, and many more.

How are your tutors selected?

All our tutors go through a rigorous selection process including background checks, teaching certifications, language proficiency tests, and interviews.

What if I'm not satisfied with my tutor?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your tutor, you can switch to another one at no additional cost.

Do you offer corporate plans?

Yes! We offer customized corporate language training programs for businesses. Contact our sales team for more information.

Need Immediate Help?

Our live chat support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any questions.
